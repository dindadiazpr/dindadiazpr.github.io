Nice to meet you

A teacher who still gets excited about gold stars.

I am an English Literature graduate from Udayana University, and for the past few years I have been helping people build confidence in English and enjoy the learning process. I teach students of all ages and backgrounds, and I enjoy creating lessons that are engaging, practical, and enjoyable.

Young learners hold a special place in my heart, and they are where I do my best work. For children, English is not just a subject. It is something we explore through songs, stories, games, and creativity. I adapt every lesson to the child in front of me, including children with special educational needs, so they can learn in a way that feels comfortable, meaningful, and fun.

I also teach teenagers, adults, and English for Specific Purposes. I am comfortable helping students prepare for exams, improve their communication skills, or use English confidently in their careers. I have experience teaching at an international school in Bali, and I would love to continue growing in an international learning environment, whether in a school or an education startup where English is used every day.

Come on in

Inside my classroom

Phonics & Letters

We sound out letters with actions, flashcards, and silly voices until the alphabet feels like a friend. D is for drum, B is for bat, and little hands trace, build, and spell their very first English words.

Story Time

Big pictures, funny voices, and "what happens next?" Stories and gentle screen time grow vocabulary and imagination at once, and they are every class's favorite moment.

Songs & Play

Hello songs, counting chants, movement games, and hands-on projects like growing our own bean sprouts. When children sing, move, and discover, English slips in without anyone noticing they are learning.

Show & Tell

Each child brings something they love and tells us about it in English. It builds confidence, listening, and the joy of being heard.
